About Us

WOF Wooden is a furniture company whoose work is inspired by Japanese design style. Minimalist yet detailed. Our main concern lies in designing flat-pack furniture.

Story

Start a journey of producing wooden letters and home decor since 2016, WOF Wooden has now switched to furniture products. Had a hiatus from 2018 to early 2020, then reactivated with a new look and vision followed by launch products in June 2020.
